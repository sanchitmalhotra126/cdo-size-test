2015Alumnae Association Annual Report
To remain in “good standing” status with Alpha Xi Delta Fraternity, alumnae associations are requiredto submit this Alumnae
Association Annual Report packet and National Alumnae Dues on behalf of their members each year. These two items must be
received by Fraternity Headquarters by May 1 in order for an alumnae association to be eligible for the Convention
travel stipend, voting privileges at National Convention and Fraternity awards.

not be saved when you close your document, and youwill lose all your typed information. If you are using a
Mac, please be sure to open the document in Adobe Reader and not the Preview option, which is typically
the default option.

Headquarters.
